Who We Are

Rooted in Scripture and shaped by classical tradition, we seek to raise a generation of wise, courageous leaders for the renewal of Englewood.

From Mending to Aiming

Living and working in Englewood means becoming intimately familiar with a grief that should not be. I have stood with too many families around caskets that should have been graduation stages.

For over 25 years through Englewood Family Outreach, our ministry has lovingly mended the arrows that have come our way. That work is vital, but we were often intervening mid-flight.

The thought hit me: What if our calling was not just to fix, but to aim?

This vision is Archer Christian Academy. We are taking on the role of the Archer—the steady hand that chooses a better target, draws the bow with discipline, and ensures a truer aim from the very start.


— Daniel M. Bair, Executive Director

Our Mission

To forge students as whole persons—mind, body, and soul—through a Christ-centered classical education.

Our Vision

To see Englewood renewed by the hope of the Gospel, lived out by generations of servant leaders who build flourishing families, churches, and communities.

Statement of Faith

We are unapologetically Christian. Our faith is not confined to a Bible class but is the foundation of everything we do.

On God

We believe in one all-powerful God who rules over all things for His glory. He has eternally existed as three persons in perfect unity: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit.

On Creation & Humanity

We believe God created humanity, male and female, in His own image. Every person bears that image and carries deep worth, dignity, and purpose. We affirm the sanctity of all human life and the beauty of God's design for marriage.

On Sin

We believe that when humanity first sinned, it fractured everything—corrupting our hearts, our relationships, and the world around us. This brokenness is the root of the conflict and darkness we see in our community.

On Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully man. He lived a sinless life, died on the cross in our place to pay for our sins, and rose from the dead three days later. He reigns at the right hand of the Father.

On Salvation

We believe we are saved from sin and made right with God by grace alone, through faith in Jesus Christ alone. This salvation is a free gift not earned by our efforts.

On The Holy Spirit & The Church

We believe the Holy Spirit brings believers from death to life, giving us new hearts. The Church is God's chosen family, called to make disciples and carry out His mission in the world.

On The Bible

We believe the Bible, from Genesis to Revelation, is the inspired and perfect Word of God. It stands as the ultimate authority for what we believe and how we live.

On The Future

We believe Christ will return to judge the world and bring His eternal kingdom to completion, fully renewing the heavens and the earth. This hope calls us to labor for the peace and restoration of our city.
